Consistency vs Transparency:
Lessons from Q3 Earnings

AUTHORS

Zach Katz

Alex Latushkin

Ekaterina Stankova

 

After four straight quarters of YoY profit declines, the Q3 2023 earnings season marked a return to profit growth, and a continued trend of revenue and earnings upside to consensus estimates.

In exploring the theme and magnitude of whether investors reward consistency or transparency in guidance, the Consello Research team assessed the differences in performances of stocks across multiple EPS guidance revision scenarios.



"Our findings show companies that maintain annual forecasts through all four quarters saw high YTD share price gains (+28%) by a wide margin. However, companies that lowered expectations at any point underperformed, even including those that guided up following a lowered bar - with losses as high as -18% YTD among serial downward adjusters."

Consello Research Team
